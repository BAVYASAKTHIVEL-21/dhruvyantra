# ✨DhruvYantra✨

### Your AI-Powered Guiding Star for JEE & NEET Preparation

DhruvYantra is an AI-powered study companion designed to help JEE and NEET aspirants stay organized, focused, and consistent throughout their preparation journey.

Built on **Coral**, DhruvYantra unifies study planning, AI mentorship, resource management, scheduling, and notifications through a single query-driven architecture.

---

## Problem Statement

Students preparing for competitive exams often rely on multiple disconnected tools for:

* Study planning
* Revision scheduling
* Resource management
* Progress tracking
* Parent communication

Managing these platforms separately creates friction and reduces focus on actual learning.

---

## Solution

DhruvYantra brings the entire preparation journey into a single platform by providing:

* Personalized onboarding
* Dynamic study plans
* AI-powered mentorship
* Deep focus sessions
* Study resource vault
* Parent progress tracking
* Smart reminders and notifications

---

## How Coral Powers DhruvYantra

Coral serves as the integration layer connecting multiple services through a unified query interface.

Instead of managing individual APIs, DhruvYantra leverages Coral to:

* Store and retrieve user profiles
* Generate and manage study plans
* Schedule calendar events
* Access study resources
* Send Telegram notifications
* Power AI mentor interactions

---

## Key Features

### Personalized Onboarding

Captures exam goals, weak topics, study habits, and preferences to personalize the learning experience.

### Mission Control Dashboard

Provides daily missions, revision plans, weak-topic tracking, and progress insights.

### Deep Focus Mode

Structured focus sessions with timers, streak tracking, and study queues.

### AI Mentor

Provides personalized study recommendations and guidance based on student progress.

### Parent Connect

Delivers daily and weekly progress updates through Telegram.

### Study Vault

Centralized access to notes, PDFs, PYQs, and learning resources stored in Google Drive.
